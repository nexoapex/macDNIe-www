3.What DNIe for Mac is and what you need

DNIe for Mac lets you read the data and photograph on your DNIe, identify yourself with it on online services, and sign documents electronically (PAdES, CAdES, XAdES). To use it you need a Mac running macOS 13 or later, a compatible contact card reader, a working DNIe and its PIN. Operation depends on elements outside nexoapex's control — the state of your card and its certificates, government services, browsers and macOS itself — which may change without notice.
